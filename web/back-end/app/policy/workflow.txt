Workflow:

1. All unauthenticated and authenticated users can check the policy and terms.

2. Authenticated users can send requests with date and review that is alleged to infringe.

3. Authenticated users can send notices with date and review that is alleged to infringe.

4. Authenticated users can send disputes with date and review that is being disputed.

5. Administrators can check all reviews and disable display of reviews with alleged copyright violations.

6. Any contested content is restored.
